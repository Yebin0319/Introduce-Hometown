♡History

Prior to 1914

Bakdal-dong in Manan-gu and Seoksu-dong in Manan-gu belonged to Siheung City, and Anyang-dong and Dongan-gu in Manan-gu belonged to Gwacheon City.


1914~1963

Due to the consolidation of Bugun-myeon, part of Gwacheon was integrated into Seoi-myeon, Siheung-gun, and Seoi-myeon was renamed Anyang-myeon in 1941.


1963~

Bakdal-dong and Seoksu-dong were incorporated into the town of Anyang, and were promoted to the city of Anyang in 1973.

♡Fun Facts

  1. Anyang KGC Ginseng Corporation's basketball team

    Anyang KGC Ginseng Corporation's basketball team's home stadium🙌 is in Anyang, and it is a team that boasts the top performance in the KBL.



  2. Pyeongchon Hagwon Street

    The Pyeongchon Hagwon Street in Anyang is one of the most famous educational districts🖉 outside of Seoul, where many students devote themselves to their studies.



  3. Anyangcheon Stream

    Anyangcheon Stream 👣, which crosses Anyang, is an important natural resting place for its citizens. With well-established bike paths and trails, it is a place where many people spend their free time.



  4. Anyang Art High School

    Anyang has Anyang Art High School💓, where art students are studying by developing artistic talents. The school is known as a prestigious school that many artists and celebrities have gone through.
